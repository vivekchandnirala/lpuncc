ENROLLMENT PROCEDURE

If you want to join the NCC at Lovely Professional University (LPU), here is a step-by-step guide to help you understand the enrollment process in a simple way:

  1. Check Eligibility
    • Students interested in joining NCC should meet the eligibility criteria (age, physical fitness, and availability).
    • Only a limited number of seats are available, so apply early.
  2. Application Submission
    • Collect the NCC Enrollment Form from the LPU-NCC office or download it from the university's website.
    • Fill out the form with accurate information and attach any required documents (identity proof, academic records).
  3. Submit to the NCC Office
    • Submit the completed form to the NCC Office in the university.
    • The NCC Office will verify your details and keep you informed about further steps.
  4. Screening and Selection
    • After form submission, students may be required to undergo a screening process, which might include a physical fitness test and an interview.
    • Selection is based on overall performance and the number of available seats.
  5. Approval and Finalization
    • Selected students will be notified and must complete any remaining formalities.
    • Once approved, your name will be added to the LPU-NCC Cadet List.
  6. Attend Orientation and Training
    • New cadets are required to attend an orientation session to understand the NCC rules, activities, and their responsibilities.
    • Regular parades, training sessions, and meetings will begin as per the schedule shared by the NCC Office.

    For Existing NCC Students:

    • In case of any changes in the enrollment or shifting of students, approval will be taken from the NCC HQ.
    • If any existing cadet wishes to change their unit or withdraw, they must inform the ANO and complete the necessary paperwork.
