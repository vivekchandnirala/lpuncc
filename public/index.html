About LPU-NCC

LPU-NCC (Lovely Professional University - National Cadet Corps) is a prestigious wings dedicated to shaping disciplined, confident, and patriotic future leaders. We are here to make a difference by providing rigorous training in drills, physical endurance, weapon handling, map reading, and survival skills. Cadets are given the opportunity to participate in various State, National, and International camps, including the Republic Day Camp (RDC), Thal Sainik Camp (TSC), and Adventure Camps, Youth Exchange Program, which build their confidence and resilience. With a strong focus on character building and community service, LPU-NCC actively participates in social initiatives, disaster relief, and awareness programs. Under the mentorship of experienced leaders, we aim to groom cadets to excel not only in defense services but in all leadership roles, making LPU-NCC a symbol of excellence, discipline, and national pride.

Pledge

We the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.

Company and squadron in LPU-NCC

The organization comprises two main squadrons: the Army Wing and the Air Force Wing, with a total of four companies under their structure. These include the 2PB Boys, 8PB, 2PB Girls, and the Air squad. The total vacancies across all units amount to 542, with a distribution of 107 cadets in the 2PB Boys, 370 cadets in the 8PB, 55 cadets in the 2PB Girls, and 10 cadets in the Air Scods. These members form a diverse and dynamic group that supports the overall functioning of the respective wings, ensuring a balanced representation and participation in both army and air force activities. Leading this dedicated team are Lt (Dr) Kamaljeet Singh, the ANOs (Associate NCC Officers), who provide strategic guidance and mentorship to the cadets, fostering a disciplined and harmonious environment within the NCC. Their leadership plays a vital role in shaping the future of the organization.
